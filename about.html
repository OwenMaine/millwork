Our Story

Jacksonville's Premier Custom Millwork Specialists

Located in the heart of Jacksonville, Superior Millwork Company has been crafting exceptional custom cabinets and millwork for discerning clients since establishment. Our state-of-the-art facility at 501 East 27th Street houses our skilled craftsmen and advanced woodworking equipment, allowing us to create stunning custom pieces that perfectly match our clients' visions.

From elegant kitchen cabinets to sophisticated entertainment centers, our team combines traditional woodworking techniques with modern technology to deliver superior quality and attention to detail in every project.
